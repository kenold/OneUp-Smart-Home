What is Smart Home or Home Automation Technology?

Smart Home Technology, also known as Home Automation, gives you access to control devices in your home from a mobile device anywhere in the world. Smart Home Technology also provides home owners comfort, security, energy efficiency and convenience at all times.

Examples of smart home technologies.

Smart Plugs can turn your lights, ceiling fans, and other fixtures off and on no matter where you are. They can even tell you how much power or energy you use on your electronics, giving you valuable insight into which electronics and appliances are costing you the most each month.

With smart security cameras, home owners can monitor their homes when they are away or on vacation. Smart motion sensors can identify the difference between residents, visitors, pets and burglars, and can notify authorities if suspicious behavior is detected.

Smart Lighting systems replace your old incandescent or halogen bulbs with LEDs. LED bulbs not only ensure a saving on electricity consumption but also use less energy, produce less heat and last much longer.
